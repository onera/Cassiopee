CPlot : Anaglyphs

In Cassiopee module CPlot, Red/Cyan anaglyph mode is triggered by: CPlot.setState(stereo=1).
In tkCassiopee interface, anaglyph can be triggered in View:tkStereo.
Red/Cyan glasses are required!

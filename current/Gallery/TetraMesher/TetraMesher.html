Generator : TETRA mesher

Generator function G.tetraMesher, based on netgen or tetgen can be used to generate a TETRA mesh inside a trangulated surface. This function is also available from the GUI in Mesh::tkTetraMesher.
         
              Volume TETRA mesh inside a cat.

         
              Volume TETRA mesh inside a airplane fuselage.
